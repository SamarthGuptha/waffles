The Revolution of the Three

"Whatever that means, however you got on that mountain, why not try to climb it? And do it in your own way."

Stephen Curry didn't just play basketball; he rewrote the manual. Before 2012, the 3 pointer was a tool; after Curry, it became the primary weapon. His ability to shoot off the dribble from Logo Range forced defenses to extend further than ever before in NBA history. This "gravity" opened up the floor for his teammates, creating the dynasty known as the Golden State Warriors. His 2016 season remains the only unanimous MVP selection in league history, a testament to a level of dominance that transcended simple statistics.

Career Milestones

All-Time 3 Point Leader

On Dec 14, 2021, at Madison Square Garden, Steph passed Ray Allen for the most career 3-pointers made in regular season history.

The 50-40-90 Club

In 2016, Steph joined the elite club of players who shot at least 50% from the field, 40% from three, and 90% from the free throw line while leading the league in scoring.

Olympic Gold 2024

The "Night Night" heard 'round the world. Curry's late game heroics against France secured his first Olympic Gold Medal in Paris.
